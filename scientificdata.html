Homeless Population in Los Angeles

  • Fact 1: Over half a million people are homeless.

  • Fact 2: One quarter of homeless people are children.

  • Fact 3: Tens of thousands of veterans are homeless.

  • Fact 4: About 44,359 people sleep on the streets & in shelters every night.

  • Fact 5: Domestic violence is a leading cause of homelessness among women.

  • Fact 6: Many people are homeless because they cannot afford rent.

  • Fact 7: Families comprise nearly 40% of all who are homeless.

  • Fact 8: 42% of homeless children are under the age of 6.

  • Fact 9: There has been a 12% increase in homelessness since last year (2018).

  • Fact 10: 9% of homeless people are under age 18.
