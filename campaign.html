Homeless Population in Los Angeles

Problem:

The homeless population in Los Angeles is rapidly increasing! The amount of people living on the streets of L.A. is growing by the day and it is effecting everyone.

Solution:

The goverment can needs to make a change in the costs of living, so that it can be more affordable to the people in the lower class. It is vital that the city of Los Angeles finds a way to get people off of the streets and into suitable housing.

Action:

An action that we can all do to make a difference in our city's crisis is donating money to local homeless shelters.
